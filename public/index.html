⚔️ How to read the battle · 怎么看懂这场仗

🗡️ Boss — your current quest in this project. Forged from your prompt; its size/tier comes from the estimated token cost. Boss=当前任务,体型=预估 token 量。

❤️ Boss HP — falls as todos get checked off. At 0 the boss kneels (☠ broken); it dies automatically when the session stops. todo 全勾=濒死,停机自动击杀,免打字。

🟢 Minions — the todo list. Each completed todo drains a slime's HP to zero. 小怪=todo,做完一只死一只。

⚡ Token — your resource (5h rate window). Rest restores it. Token=你的资源,休息回复。

🔥 Combo — consecutive successful tool strikes. 连击=连续成功操作。

🍖 Feeding — while planning, every plan update and answered question feeds the boss and it grows. 计划和问答越喂越大。

🐺 Summons — subagent dispatches fight beside the knight. 召唤兽=subagent。

💰 Gold — real session cost (USD). ⚔️ weapon = model, 🗡️ ATK = lines changed, ⏳ session time, 🏕️ weekly quota. 金币=本会话花费,武器=模型,ATK=改动行数。

Commands: /slime:arena · /slime:defeat · /slime:milestones · /slime:wrapped
